OUR DEPARTMENTS


FOR MORE INFO PLACE YOUR MOUSE IN ANY VIDEO YOU WANT

Kitchen

The kitchen department is the heart of the hotel's culinary operations. It's responsible for preparing, cooking, and presenting a variety of dishes to meet the needs of guests. The kitchen staff, including chefs and cooks, work in a meticulously organized environment to ensure the quality and taste of the food served in the hotel's restaurants, cafes, and banquet halls.

Gym

The gym department focuses on promoting health and wellness for hotel guests. Equipped with modern exercise machines and facilities, the gym offers a space for guests to engage in physical activities, workouts, and fitness routines. Certified trainers may be available to assist guests, offering guidance and personalized exercise plans to cater to different fitness goals.

Halls

The halls department oversees event spaces and conference facilities within the hotel. These spaces are used for meetings, conferences, weddings, and other gatherings. The department is responsible for setting up the event space according to the client's requirements, providing audiovisual equipment, coordinating catering services, and ensuring that events run smoothly

Spa

The spa department provides relaxation and rejuvenation for hotel guests. It offers a range of wellness services, including massages, facials, body treatments, and more. The spa is designed to provide a tranquil and soothing atmosphere, allowing guests to unwind and destress. Licensed therapists and professionals deliver personalized treatments to enhance guests' physical and mental well-being.
